## Selected work

### Vera — Identity verification

**Product Design · UX flows · Functional prototype**

A guided identity-verification experience designed to make document capture, instructions, validation and recovery states easier to understand. The project connects UX decisions with an implementation-ready interface.

[Explore the Vera case →](https://github.com/suquediego/vera-case-certta)

### Medical Residency — Adaptive study experience

**Product strategy · Mobile UX · Metrics · Prototyping**

A mobile-first product that helps medical students answer a simple but important question: *what should I study next?* The experience connects a daily plan, question sessions, feedback and performance data into clear next steps.

[Explore the product and documentation →](https://github.com/suquediego/residencia-medica-mvp)

## About me

I design end-to-end experiences for complex digital products, especially fintech, identity verification, operational dashboards and B2B platforms.

My process goes from discovery and journey mapping to high-fidelity prototypes, validation, Design Systems and developer handoff. I also use AI to explore alternatives, accelerate prototyping and turn design decisions into functional interfaces.
